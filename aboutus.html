About Us

Ideal Public School, located in Talheri Buzurg, Saharanpur, is a growing educational institution committed to academic excellence and holistic development. Affiliated with the CBSE curriculum, we provide a safe, supportive, and engaging environment where students are encouraged to grow intellectually, emotionally, and socially. Our school is backed by a well-trained and experienced faculty, modern security through CCTV monitoring, and a strong emphasis on co-curricular development including indoor and outdoor sports. Personality development programs further ensure that each child becomes confident, capable, and compassionate.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver excellence in education by combining academic rigor with moral and social responsibility. We are committed to building a future where students not only excel in their studies but also grow into thoughtful, confident individuals who serve society with integrity. Through continuous innovation, dedicated mentorship, and a focus on all-round development, we aim to shape young minds to lead and inspire.

Our Aim

Our aim is to nurture responsible global citizens through quality education and value-based learning. We strive to empower every student to reach their highest potential by providing the right guidance, resources, and support. Ideal Public School is dedicated to creating a dynamic and inclusive learning atmosphere that fosters curiosity, creativity, and character.
